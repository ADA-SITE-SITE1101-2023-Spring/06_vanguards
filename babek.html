We played football, basketball, skated and so on. I was very noisy and hyperactive. In addition, I was very interested in encyclopedias. At the age of 7-8 years, I had already finished 2 encyclopedias. Facts about plants, animals and especially astronomy were very interesting to me at the age of 8-9. My early age was quite interesting and developing."

Career

"When we moved to Azerbaijan, I went to school number 177 and graduated from it. Since 6th grade I started to plan my future job and career. Firstly, I decided to be a lawyer. FInally, at 8th grade I completely made my mind about my job: computer scientist. I studied hard and wanted all my university classes to be in English. That's one of the main reasons why I entered the ADA University. Here I study as a computer science student and learn everything about programming for my future job. I hope I will be a demanded person in the future."
